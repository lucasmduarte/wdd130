Family Rafting

The newest RAFTING itinerary with the aim of a nature experience for children, consists of going down a route on the Paranhana River with calmer rapids and lots of fun. This itinerary is recommended for families with children between 4 and 7 years old, an interaction between parents, children and grandparents in a rafting experience. In Family Rafting the route is shorter, but the fun is guaranteed in the same way. The excitement is for the whole family and/or group of friends. The activities of boat surfing on the wave, relaxing bath, jumping from the platform and WATER ZIP LINE are also included in this itinerary.

Rafting

For more than two decades, RAFTING has been the best-known adventure attraction in the Paranhana Valley! Come be enchanted and navigate the rapids of the Paranhana River, at the foot of the Serra Gaúcha, discovering a beautiful and lush forest, fun rapids, a different look at the beautiful Atlantic Forest. In the largest and most complete Rafting itinerary in Três Coroas, you will have fun along 8 km of rapids with adventure and lots of fun from start to finish. The excitement is for the whole family, companies and group of friends. Here, at American Rafting, we take you far beyond just one adventure! Along with Rafting, you also have other attractions such as the exclusive Aquatic Zipline and the super platform jump! and also the relaxing bath and boat surfing on the wave. Night Rafting

Who said adventures are only to be had in the sunlight? Come enjoy a beautiful night of stars and a full moon on this unforgettable tour! The itinerary is the same as the daytime activity, 8km of rapids with fun from start to finish. At night the senses are heightened and the adventure intensifies. Without the intervention of artificial lights, this is the perfect setting to embark on a completely new experience! Come challenge your senses with us! Minimum age: 15 years old accompanied by parents
